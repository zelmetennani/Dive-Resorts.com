Diving in Thailand

Thailand offers some of the best diving in Southeast Asia, with crystal clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and an abundance of marine life. From the Andaman Sea to the Gulf of Thailand, divers can explore a variety of underwater environments.

Whether you're looking to spot whale sharks at Richelieu Rock, explore the famous dive sites around the Similan Islands, or enjoy the convenience of shore diving in Koh Tao, Thailand has something for every diver.

Best Time to Visit

November to April (Andaman Sea), March to September (Gulf of Thailand)

Dive Experience

All levels, with many sites suitable for beginners

Marine Life

Whale sharks, manta rays, reef sharks, turtles, and over 1,000 species of fish

Popular Dive Sites in Thailand

Richelieu Rock

Famous for whale shark sightings and vibrant soft corals. One of Thailand's premier dive sites.

Depth: 5-35m | Level: Intermediate to Advanced

Similan Islands

Crystal clear waters with exceptional visibility, boulder formations, and diverse marine life.

Depth: 5-40m | Level: All levels

Sail Rock, Koh Tao

Pinnacle rising from the depths, known for schools of barracuda and occasional whale shark sightings.

Depth: 8-30m | Level: Intermediate

Shark Point, Phi Phi

Named for its leopard sharks, this site also features colorful soft corals and abundant marine life.

Depth: 10-25m | Level: Intermediate
