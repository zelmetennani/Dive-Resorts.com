Diving in Mexico

Mexico offers some of the most diverse diving experiences in the world. From the vibrant reefs of the Caribbean to the rich marine biodiversity of the Sea of Cortez, Mexico is a diver's paradise.

The Mesoamerican Reef, the second largest barrier reef system in the world, runs along Mexico's Caribbean coast, providing incredible opportunities for both novice and experienced divers.

Best Time to Visit

Year-round, with peak visibility from May to September

Dive Experience

All levels, from beginner to advanced

Marine Life

Whale sharks, manta rays, sea turtles, reef sharks

Popular Dive Sites in Mexico

Palancar Reef, Cozumel

Famous for its dramatic drop-offs and vibrant coral formations. Perfect for drift diving.

Depth: 12-40m | Level: Intermediate to Advanced

MUSA Underwater Museum, Cancun

Artificial reef with over 500 sculptures, creating a unique diving experience.

Depth: 3-8m | Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Cenote Dos Ojos, Riviera Maya

One of the most spectacular cave systems in the world, with crystal clear freshwater.

Depth: 5-10m | Level: Beginner to Advanced

Cabo Pulmo Marine Reserve

Protected area with the highest concentration of marine life in the Sea of Cortez.

Depth: 5-30m | Level: All levels
